Common questions

Everything you need to know

Do I need gym experience?

No. JP Fit is beginner-friendly and every plan starts at your current ability.

Where can mobile training happen?

At your home gym, apartment or condo gym, HOA fitness center, or another approved private training space.

What is online coaching?

You train at your own location while receiving custom workouts, nutrition guidance, check-ins, progress tracking, and messaging through the JP Fit app.

Do I need an extreme diet?

No. JP Fit focuses on sustainable nutrition habits that fit your lifestyle.

Can exercises be modified?

Many exercises can be adjusted around common limitations. Medical clearance may be required when appropriate.
