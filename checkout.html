1. Parties and Program

This Coaching Agreement (“Agreement”) is between JP Fit (“Coach”) and the Client. The Client is enrolling in a JP Fit program, at the selected frequency, for a biweekly payment of the selected price. The selected term is the selected agreement, beginning on the selected start date.

2. Services

Depending on the selected program, services may include personal training sessions, individualized workout programming, general nutrition guidance, accountability check-ins, progress tracking, exercise instruction, and reasonable coaching support. Specific schedules and deliverables may be adjusted by mutual agreement.

3. Automatic Biweekly Payments

The Client authorizes the payment method entered through Stripe to be charged every 14 days at the selected rate. A six-month term generally includes approximately 13 payments, and a twelve-month term generally includes approximately 26 payments. Declined payments must be corrected promptly. Training access may be suspended while an account is past due.

4. Agreement Term and Cancellation

Cancellation requests must be submitted in writing to jeremyparr18@gmail.com. Fixed-term agreements do not automatically renew into another fixed term. Any continuation after the initial fixed term will require the Client’s affirmative agreement or a separate month-to-month arrangement.

5. Downgrade Policy

Six-month agreement: After the first 60 days, the Client may request one reduction in training frequency during the term. Twelve-month agreement: After the first 90 days, the Client may request up to two reductions in frequency during the term. Downgrades require written approval, take effect on a future billing cycle, do not shorten the agreement, and change future charges to the current rate for the lower frequency.

6. Early Termination

A Client ending a six- or twelve-month agreement before the scheduled end date must provide written notice and pay an early termination fee equal to one final biweekly payment at the Client’s then-current rate. JP Fit may waive or modify the fee in writing for documented medical inability, permanent relocation, death, or another exceptional circumstance.

7. Sessions, Rescheduling, and Expiration

  • At least 24 hours’ notice is required to cancel or reschedule a live session.
  • Late cancellations and no-shows may result in forfeiture of that session.
  • Emergencies may be reviewed at JP Fit’s discretion.
  • Unused sessions generally expire within the applicable billing period unless JP Fit approves an extension in writing.
  • JP Fit may reschedule a session and will provide a replacement opportunity when reasonably possible.

8. Refunds

Payments are nonrefundable once processed except where required by law or expressly approved by JP Fit in writing. Completed services and forfeited sessions are nonrefundable.

9. Health Disclosure and Assumption of Risk

The Client confirms that they have disclosed relevant injuries, health conditions, medications, restrictions, and physician instructions. Exercise carries inherent risks, including strains, falls, cardiovascular events, and other injury. The Client voluntarily participates, agrees to use reasonable judgment, and will stop and report concerning symptoms. Medical clearance may be requested. JP Fit does not diagnose, treat, prescribe, or provide medical care.

10. Liability and Client Responsibility

To the fullest extent permitted by law, the Client assumes the ordinary risks of voluntary exercise participation and agrees that JP Fit is not responsible for injury or loss caused by undisclosed conditions, failure to follow instructions, misuse of equipment, unsafe conduct, or circumstances outside JP Fit’s reasonable control. This provision does not waive rights that cannot legally be waived.

11. Nutrition Scope

Nutrition support is general educational guidance and habit coaching. It is not medical nutrition therapy or treatment for disease. Clients with medical or dietary conditions should work with a physician or appropriately licensed nutrition professional.

12. Media Permission

Marketing permission is optional and separate from coaching. The Client’s choice below does not affect eligibility for services. A Client may withdraw permission for future uses by written notice; withdrawal does not require removal of materials already lawfully published or distributed.

13. Electronic Records and Signatures

The parties agree to conduct this transaction electronically. The Client intends the typed signature and acceptance boxes below to serve as an electronic signature. The Client should save or print a copy of this Agreement. This website version creates a client-side record; JP Fit should use an approved electronic-signature platform or secure backend for production recordkeeping.

14. Entire Agreement and Changes

This document, the selected membership summary, and any incorporated written policies form the agreement between the parties. Changes must be in writing. If one provision is unenforceable, the remaining provisions continue to the extent permitted by law. Florida law governs, subject to any mandatory consumer rights.
