JP FIT VERSION 24 — CUSTOM TEMPLATE FLOW

NO NEW SQL REQUIRED.

1. Open Coach Dashboard.
2. Go to Workout Builder.
3. Click + Custom Template.
4. Enter workout name, type, and optional summary.
5. Click Start Building.
6. Add exercises from the Exercise Library.
7. Edit descriptions and rest guidance.
8. Click Save as My Template.

Saved templates appear in My Workout Templates with Open and Delete buttons.
