JP FIT GOOGLE ANALYTICS 4 SETUP

1. Go to analytics.google.com.
2. Create an Analytics account and a GA4 property for JP Fit.
3. Create a Web data stream for:
   https://www.trainwithjpfit.com
4. Copy the Measurement ID. It begins with:
   G-
5. Open analytics-config.js in the Version 1.0.3 files.
6. Replace:
   G-XXXXXXXXXX
   with your real Measurement ID.
7. Upload the updated analytics-config.js to GitHub and commit.
8. Open the website and check Google Analytics -> Realtime.

The site automatically sends standard page views after the ID is added.
It also includes launch-funnel events for agreement and intake submission starts.

Do not send health information, agreement contents, names, email addresses, or payment information as analytics event values.
